The sculpture I made is a yin and yang symbol. The yin and yang symbol shows a balance between two opposites with a portion of the opposite element in each section. The yin, the dark swirl, is associated with shadows, femininity, and the trough of a wave. Culturally, the yin and yang symbol comes from Taoism/Daoism which is a Chinese religion and philosophy. The yang, the light swirl, represents brightness, passion and growth. I chose to create this because it has had a great significance in my life. Balance is extremely important to me and this is why I chose the yin and yang symbol.
